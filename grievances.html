Grievance Redressal

We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly and fairly

How to File a Grievance

If you have any concerns, complaints, or grievances regarding our services, please contact us through the following channels:

Primary Contact

  • Email: Gautamkathuria0909@gmail.com
  • Subject Line: Please use "Grievance - [Brief Description]" for faster processing

Response Timeline

We aim to respond to all grievances within 7 working days from the date of receipt of your complaint.

You will receive an acknowledgment of your grievance within 2 working days, followed by a detailed response within the 7-day timeline.

Information to Include

To help us address your grievance effectively, please include:

  • Your full name and contact details
  • Date of the incident or concern
  • Detailed description of the grievance
  • Any relevant documents or correspondence
  • Your preferred resolution outcome

Escalation to SEBI SCORES

If you are not satisfied with our response or resolution, you may escalate your complaint to SEBI SCORES (after SEBI registration is complete).

  • SEBI SCORES Portal: https://scores.gov.in
  • Note: SEBI SCORES escalation is available after SEBI registration is obtained. Current registration status is mentioned in the Disclosures page.

Our Commitment

We take all grievances seriously and are committed to:

  • Providing timely acknowledgment and response
  • Conducting fair and thorough investigations
  • Offering appropriate resolutions
  • Maintaining confidentiality throughout the process
  • Learning from feedback to improve our services
