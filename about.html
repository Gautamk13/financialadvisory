My Story

As a working professional with experience in finance, I have managed my personal investments and helped family members navigate their financial decisions. Through this experience, I have observed the common challenges that individuals face when they have income but lack proper financial education—the pressure to start investing immediately, confusion from conflicting advice, and the absence of a structured approach to financial planning.

Having seen these problems firsthand, I recognized the need for goal-based, fee-only financial advice that prioritizes client education and long-term discipline over quick tips or product sales. This led me to establish this advisory practice, focused on helping young professionals build financial confidence through knowledge and thoughtful planning.

Investment Philosophy

Investing requires discipline, knowledge, and proper goal-based investing. Successful wealth creation is not about chasing returns or following market trends—it's about building a structured approach that aligns with your financial objectives, risk tolerance, and time horizon. Goal-based investing ensures that every investment decision serves a specific purpose, whether it's retirement planning, wealth accumulation, or achieving short-term milestones. This systematic approach, combined with disciplined execution and continuous learning, forms the foundation of sustainable financial growth.
